## 3. Outliers & Takeaways

- **Best value drop**: **Mighty Mutanimals** ($47.71 in singles) — Triumph of the Hordes and Pirated Copy carry this hard.
- **Worst value drop**: **VHS Villains** ($23.68) — Higure and Species Specialist hold some value, but Delver/Baloths/Triskelion are bulk.
- **Most playable drop overall**: **The Last Ronin** — Mental Misstep, Chord of Calling, Anger, and Ink-Eyes are all genuinely good cards across multiple strategies.
- **Biggest price vs. playability disconnect**: **Sakashima's Student** ($28.56) is expensive purely due to scarcity (only one printing), not because it's widely played. **Master of Ceremonies** ($19.56) is similar — pricey but niche.
- **Sleeper hit**: **Guttersnipe** at $0.26 is one of the most-played cards in the entire superdrop (EDHREC #400). Pure value if you're a spellslinger player.
- **Delver of Secrets** is the ultimate format outlier — one of the most iconic cards in competitive 60-card formats but nearly useless in Commander.
